问路

Excuse me, can you tell me how to get to LAX?

Excuse me, do you know where Forerver 21 is?
Excuse me, do you know where this location is?

How long is it gonna take (for me) to get there?
How long is the walk?

uber司机

I’m at (Valley) and (Hellman). 我在valley和hellman两条街交口处。
I’m in front of the (Toyota) Building.
I’m across the street from (Mcdonalds).我在麦当劳对面。
I’m at the intersection.我在十字路口。

Go straight. Make a left when you see Mcdonalds. And then go strait. When you see a coffee shop, make a right.

I’m right by (the Freeway).我就在高速旁边。
I’m right by the Mcdonals on Hellman and Valley.我就在helloman和valley交接处的麦当劳哪里。

Are you the (blue) (Toyota Prius)? 你是那辆蓝色的丰田prius吗？
I’m wearing… I’m standing right in front of the Taxi line.我就站在出租车排队前。
